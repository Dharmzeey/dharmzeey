# Kwiseworld Inventory

**Kwiseworld Inventory** is a powerful inventory management system designed to help businesses maintain accurate stock records, process sales efficiently, and gain valuable insights through comprehensive reporting.

The system combines modern web technologies with practical business logic to deliver a reliable and user-friendly inventory management solution.

---

## Features

### Dashboard & Overview
A comprehensive dashboard providing real-time insights into inventory levels, recent sales, low stock alerts, and key business metrics at a glance.

---

## Inventory Management
Efficiently manage your entire product catalog with features for adding, editing, and organizing inventory items.

### **Core Features:**
- Add and manage product listings with detailed information
- Track stock levels with automatic updates
- Set reorder points and receive low stock alerts
- Categorize products for easy organization
- Barcode/SKU support for quick product lookup
- Bulk import/export capabilities

---

## Sales Processing
Streamlined point-of-sale functionality for quick and accurate transaction processing.

### **Sales Features:**
- Fast product search and selection
- Multiple payment method support
- Receipt generation
- Sales history tracking
- Customer information management
- Return and refund processing

---

## Reporting & Analytics
Generate detailed reports to understand business performance and make data-driven decisions.

### **Reporting Features:**
- Sales reports by date, product, or category
- Inventory valuation reports
- Profit and loss statements
- Low stock and reorder reports
- Custom date range filtering
- Export reports to PDF/Excel

---

## User Management
Role-based access control ensures secure operations with different permission levels.

### **User Features:**
- Multi-user support with role assignments
- Admin, manager, and staff roles
- Activity logging and audit trails
- Secure authentication

---

## Frameworks and Libraries
- Django
- Django Rest Framework
- React/Next.js
- Tailwind CSS
- PostgreSQL
